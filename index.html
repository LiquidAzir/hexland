How to Play

Goal

First to 10 Victory Points wins.

Earning Points

  • Settlement = 1 VP · City = 2 VP
  • Longest Road (5+ continuous) = 2 VP
  • Largest Army (3+ knights played) = 2 VP
  • Victory Point dev cards = 1 VP each

Turn

  1. Roll two dice — every settlement/city next to a hex of that number produces its resource (settlement = 1, city = 2)
  2. Build, trade, or play a dev card
  3. End your turn

The Robber

Rolling 7 — anyone holding more than 7 cards discards half. Then move the robber to a hex and steal a random card from one player there. While on a hex, that hex doesn't produce.

Costs

  • Road: 1 wood + 1 brick
  • Settlement: 1 wood + 1 brick + 1 sheep + 1 wheat
  • City: 2 wheat + 3 ore (upgrades a settlement)
  • Dev Card: 1 sheep + 1 wheat + 1 ore

Trading

Bank: 4 of one resource → 1 of any. Ports give 3:1 generic or 2:1 specific. Propose trades with other players too.

D-Pad

Arrow keys move focus. Enter selects. Esc/Back exits menus. When placing pieces, arrows move the highlight between valid spots; Enter confirms.
